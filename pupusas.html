Pupusas

My favorite Salvadorean dish, introduced to me a few years ago by my friend.

Ingredients

  • 4 cup plus 1/3 cup vegetable oil, divided
  • 1/2 medium white onion, halved, broken up into individual layers
  • 1 15-ounce can of pinto beans
  • kosher salt
  • 3 cups instant corn masa flour
  • 4 ounces grated queso Oaxaca
  • Salvadorean cabbage relish (curtido)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the masa.
  2. Stuff the pupusas.
  3. Cook them on the stovetop until they're ready.
